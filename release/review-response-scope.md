# Review Response Scope

**Status:** Public-safe response guidance  
**Purpose:** keep reviewer answers focused on the public-safe prototype

This note defines how to answer questions about WorkflowGuard AI during a controlled review.

---

## 1. Answer at the right level

Public review answers should stay at the level of:

```text
what the prototype is for
what the free preview shows
why synthetic examples are used
why human review matters
what a safe first experiment means
what the sample output demonstrates
what the package currently proves
what the package does not prove yet
```

---

## 2. Do not turn review answers into a full playbook

If a question asks for deeper build, operating, or business details, answer only at a high level and point back to the package index, sample output, or public-safe review note sample.

Use this short response pattern:

```text
This package is meant to explain the public-safe prototype and sample workflow-review structure. It is not meant to provide a complete operating playbook. For review purposes, please use the package index and public-safe samples.
```

---

## 3. Safe redirect targets

Use these files for public-safe answers:

```text
README.md
release/public-safe-package-index.md
examples/mini-audit-preview-sample.md
examples/public-safe-review-note-sample.md
docs/chinese-plain-language-overview.md
```

---

## 4. Decision

```text
Response scope added: public-safe high-level answers only.
```

This helps reduce the risk of over-answering during reviewer or customer-style conversations.
