## 4. Public prototype intro post draft

This is only a draft. Do not publish without owner approval.

```text
I’m building a public-safe prototype called WorkflowGuard AI.

The idea is simple:
Before a team uses AI with sensitive data or production workflows, it should first be able to answer:
- What data can AI see?
- What output can AI affect?
- Who reviews the result?
- What is the safest first test?
- How do we trace the decision?

This prototype does not require login, does not store input, does not call an AI API, and does not use real customer data.

It includes:
- a static GitHub Pages demo
- 5 synthetic workflow scenarios
- a Mini Audit Preview
- capability pack documentation
- public-safe boundary checks

Demo:
https://chuangmenghuan-wq.github.io/traceable-ai-workflow-prototype/

Repo:
https://github.com/chuangmenghuan-wq/traceable-ai-workflow-prototype

This is still a prototype, not a product launch.
I’m mainly looking for feedback on whether the idea is understandable and useful as a first-step AI workflow review.
```

繁中草稿：

```text
我正在做一個 public-safe prototype，叫做 WorkflowGuard AI。

核心想法很簡單：
在公司真的把 AI 接到敏感資料或正式流程之前，應該先回答幾個問題：
- AI 可以看到什麼資料？
- AI 的輸出會影響什麼？
- 誰要審查結果？
- 最安全的第一個測試是什麼？
- 這個決策之後能不能追蹤？

這個 prototype 不需要登入、不儲存輸入、不呼叫 AI API，也不使用真實客戶資料。

這還不是正式產品發布。
```

```text

- It avoids production, compliance, cybersecurity, legal, financial, or medical guarantees.

```
