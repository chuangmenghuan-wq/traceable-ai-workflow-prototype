# Mini Audit Visual Summary Card

This card shows a short way to summarize a mini audit result.

## Example card

```text
WorkflowGuard Mini Audit Preview

Risk level: Yellow

Data boundary:
Use fake or anonymized examples first.
Do not start with real customer records.

Human review:
AI output should stay internal until a human approves it.

Safe first experiment:
Test 10 synthetic support questions.
Let AI group repeated questions and draft internal suggestions.
A human records what needed correction.

Next step:
Run a public-safe experiment before any real workflow design.
```
