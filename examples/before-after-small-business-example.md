# Before-After Small Business Example

**Status:** Public sample example  
**Task ID:** LEAD-FLOW-003B  
**Date:** 2026-07-07

This example shows why a small first test can still be useful.

---

## Before

A small shop receives repeated questions every day.

Common questions:

```text
What time do you open?
Do you have parking nearby?
Can I reserve by phone?
Do you have this item today?
Can I pick it up later?
```

The team answers these questions one by one.

---

## Small first test

Use 10 simple example questions.

Ask AI to group similar questions into a short internal list.

A person checks the grouped result.

---

## After

The team gets a simple list:

```text
Opening hours questions
Parking questions
Reservation questions
Product availability questions
Pickup questions
```

Then the team can prepare draft answers for repeated topics.

---

## Why this helps

```text
It does not need a full system first.
It shows repeated question patterns.
It helps decide whether the workflow is worth improving.
It keeps the first test small and easy to check.
```
