# Appointment-Service Before-After Example

**Status:** Public sample example  
**Task ID:** LEAD-FLOW-003N  
**Date:** 2026-07-07

This example shows how an appointment-based service can use a small first test.

---

## Before

A service team receives repeated appointment questions.

Common questions:

```text
Are there available times this week?
Can I change my appointment?
What should I prepare before I arrive?
Where should I park?
Can I bring another person?
```

The team answers them one by one.

---

## Small first test

Use 10 simple example questions.

Ask AI to group similar appointment questions.

A person checks the grouped result.

---

## After

The team gets a simple list:

```text
Available time questions
Rescheduling questions
Preparation questions
Parking questions
Guest questions
```

Then the team can prepare clearer replies for repeated topics.

---

## Why this helps

```text
It shows repeated appointment topics.
It helps prepare clearer replies.
It keeps the first test small and easy to check.
```
