# Daily Note Before-After Example

**Status:** Public sample example  
**Task ID:** LEAD-FLOW-003Y  
**Date:** 2026-07-07

This example shows how repeated daily notes can be grouped in a small first test.

---

## Before

A team writes short daily notes in different styles.

Example notes:

```text
Late start today.
Material arrived late.
Checklist item missed.
Need follow-up tomorrow.
Please review the open item.
```

The notes are hard to scan quickly.

---

## Small first test

Use 10 simple daily notes.

Ask AI to group similar notes into short topics.

A person checks the grouped result.

---

## After

The team gets a simple list:

```text
Delay notes
Material notes
Checklist notes
Follow-up notes
Review notes
```

Then the team can see repeated topic patterns more clearly.

---

## Why this helps

```text
It makes repeated topics easier to see.
It helps the team decide what to improve first.
It keeps the first test small and easy to check.
```
